Quinoa Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 100g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions and let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, and feta cheese.
  3. Add the cooled quinoa to the bowl.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, then season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss everything together and serve chilled.

Lemon Tart

Ingredients

  • For the crust:
    • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
    • 1/4 cup sugar
    • 1 egg yolk
  • For the filling:
    • 3 lemons (zest and juice)
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 4 eggs
    • 1/2 cup heavy cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. For the crust: Mix flour and sugar, then rub in butter until crumbly. Add egg yolk and mix until dough forms. Press into a tart pan and bake for 15 minutes.
  3. For the filling: Whisk together lemon zest, juice, sugar, eggs, and cream.
  4. Pour filling into the pre-baked crust and bake for another 25-30 minutes until set.
  5. Let cool before serving.
